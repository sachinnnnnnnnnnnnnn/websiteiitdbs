Sports

Many activities are in the student activity center (SAC), which is located near Gulmohar guest house, as are the Nalanda grounds. A new sports complex is coming up on the main fields (opposite Hospital). Do note that most facilities are open during specific hours and may be reserved for certain team activities at certain times. The office of the sports officer of IIT Delhi is located in SAC

  • Badminton court (in Student’s Activity Center (SAC))
  • Table tennis courts (SAC).
  • Squash courts (SAC)
  • Olympic size swimming pool (SAC)
  • Snooker table (SAC)
  • Exercise Gym (SAC). Separate rooms for ladies and gentlemen.
  • Athletic, cricket, hockey, and football sports fields
  • Basketball courts next to the Football field
  • Synthetic and clay tennis courts (opposite Nalanda grounds)
  • Volleyball courts (near the tennis courts)
